💌 A Letter To My Brother

Happy Birthday, Ashish ❤️ It is difficult to express in words what these last four years have meant to me. From the very first day of our college journey on 29 October 2022, you became more than just a friend. We met as classmates, but somewhere along the way, you became a brother. Sharing the same room for four years meant sharing not just a space, but countless memories, conversations, struggles, celebrations, and dreams.

From morning classes to late-night discussions, from exam stress to moments of laughter, you were always there. We celebrated festivals together, decorated for pujas, participated in college functions, attended events, clicked endless photos, and turned ordinary days into unforgettable memories.

You have been someone I could trust without hesitation. Whenever I needed support, advice, or simply someone to listen, you were there. Over time, the bond became so strong that my family also came to know you, trust you, and care for you.

As our college journey comes to an end on 29 April 2026, I realize that the memories we created will stay with us forever. The room we shared, the festivals we celebrated, the decorations we made together, the trips, the photographs, the jokes, the sleepless nights, and every small moment in between have become an important chapter of my life.

Thank you for being such an incredible friend, roommate, and brother. No matter where life takes us after college, these memories and this bond will always remain special.

Wishing you success in every dream, strength in every challenge, and happiness in every moment of life.

Happy Birthday once again, brother. 🎂✨
